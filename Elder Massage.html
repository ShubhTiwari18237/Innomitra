Elder Massage

As we age, our skin undergoes significant changes, becoming thinner, fragile, and more prone to dryness. For our cherished seniors, maintaining optimal skin health becomes a crucial aspect of their overall well-being. At Innomitra, we understand the unique needs of aging skin and are proud to offer specialized Massage Therapy tailored explicitly for elder care.

Why Choose Innomitra's Massage Therapy for Seniors ?

Expertise in Gentle Techniques:

Our skilled massage therapists are trained in gentle techniques that cater specifically to the delicate nature of aging skin. With a deep understanding of the changes in skin texture and fragility, our therapists provide massages that are soothing, nurturing, and safe.

Hydration and Nourishment:

Innomitra's massage therapy focuses on promoting skin hydration and nourishment. We use specially formulated, hypoallergenic lotions and oils to moisturize dry skin, alleviating discomfort caused by conditions like diabetes or kidney disease.

Delicate and Non-Invasive Approach:

Understanding the vulnerability of senior skin, our therapists employ a delicate touch and non-invasive methods. We prioritize gentle strokes and reduced tissue engagement to prevent tears or abrasions, especially for seniors with 'parchment skin.'

Improving Circulation and Healing:

Our massages are designed to enhance blood circulation, aiding in the rejuvenation and healing of the skin. Improved circulation helps seniors maintain healthier skin and may reduce the risk of skin-related complications.

Tailored Care Plans:

Innomitra offers personalized massage therapy plans that consider each elder's skin condition, health status, and specific requirements. Our therapists adapt their techniques to suit individual needs, ensuring a comfortable and beneficial experience.

Promoting Relaxation and Comfort:

Beyond the physical benefits, our massage therapy aims to promote relaxation and emotional well-being among seniors. Gentle massage sessions provide a sense of comfort, alleviating stress and promoting a serene environment.

Innomitra's Massage Therapy for elder care is more than a routine; it's a commitment to providing nurturing and attentive care to our cherished seniors. With our specialized approach, we prioritize skin health, comfort, and the overall wellness of elders, offering a haven of relaxation and rejuvenation in the comfort of their homes.
