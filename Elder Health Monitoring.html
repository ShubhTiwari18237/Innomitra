Elder's Health Monitoring At Home

Innomitra's Elder Health Monitoring at home Services. Innomitra is committed to revolutionizing elder care by bringing advanced health monitoring services directly to the comfort of their homes. Our comprehensive health monitoring program goes beyond conventional care, incorporating cutting-edge technology to ensure elders receive the highest level of personalized attention and medical oversight.

ECG Monitoring:

Innomitra's state-of-the-art ECG monitoring service allows elders to undergo electrocardiogram assessments without leaving their homes. This invaluable tool helps detect irregularities in heart rhythms, enabling early intervention and preventing potential cardiovascular issues. With Innomitra, the peace of mind that comes with regular ECG monitoring is just a heartbeat away.

Blood Pressure Tracking:

Ensuring optimal blood pressure is crucial for overall health. Innomitra's home-based blood pressure monitoring service empowers elders to keep track of their blood pressure levels regularly. Our trained professionals utilize advanced devices to measure blood pressure accurately, providing valuable insights for timely medical interventions and personalized care.

Continuous Health Surveillance:

Innomitra's commitment to elder well-being extends beyond specific health parameters. Our continuous health surveillance involves monitoring vital signs, such as heart rate, respiratory rate, and body temperature. Real-time data allows our caregivers and healthcare professionals to identify trends and address potential health concerns promptly.

Smart Health Wearables Integration:

Innomitra seamlessly integrates smart health wearables into our monitoring services. Elders can wear these devices comfortably, enabling continuous monitoring of various health metrics. The data collected is transmitted securely to our healthcare platform, ensuring a holistic and real-time view of an elder's health status.

Remote Consultations and Alerts:

Innomitra facilitates remote consultations with healthcare professionals based on the monitored data. Should any readings deviate from the normal range or trigger predefined alerts, our team ensures swift communication with both the elder and their designated emergency contacts. This proactive approach enables timely responses and minimizes health risks.

Personalized Health Insights:

Our comprehensive health monitoring doesn't just stop at data collection. Innomitra provides elders and their families with personalized health insights derived from the monitored information. This not only fosters a deeper understanding of their health but also empowers them to make informed decisions regarding their well-being. At Innomitra, we believe that empowering elders with advanced health monitoring at home is the key to proactive and personalized care. By combining cutting-edge technology with compassionate caregiving, we strive to elevate the standard of elder health and well-being.
