Elder Physiotherapy

Embarking on the journey of recovery after surgery or hospital discharge can be challenging, especially for our beloved elders. Innomitra understands the unique needs of seniors during this crucial time and is proud to introduce our Elder Physiotherapy services designed to bring personalized care to the comfort of their homes.

Why choose Innomitra Elder Physiotherapy ?

Tailored Care Plans:

Our Specialized and experienced physiotherapists create customized care plans, considering the individual needs and health conditions of each elder. From joint mobility exercises to strength training, we ensure a comprehensive and personalized approach to recovery.

Convenience of Home Visits:

We bring the expertise of our physiotherapists directly to your doorstep. No need to navigate busy clinics or face transportation challenges; our professionals deliver compassionate care in the familiar and reassuring surroundings of your home.

Post-Surgery Rehabilitation:

Innomitra specializes in post-surgery rehabilitation, helping elders regain strength, flexibility, and mobility following surgical procedures. Our physiotherapy sessions are designed to accelerate the recovery process, promoting a quicker return to daily activities.

Holistic Approach to Healing:

Beyond physical exercises, our physiotherapists focus on the overall well-being of elders. We integrate holistic techniques to address pain management, mental health, and emotional support, fostering a positive environment for recovery.

Dedicated and Trained Professionals:

Innomitra' s team of physiotherapists undergo rigorous training and is equipped with the knowledge to address the specific requirements of elder care. Our professionals are committed to providing gentle, effective, and compassionate support.

Continuous Monitoring and Adjustment:

We believe in the importance of adapting care plans as elders progress in their recovery journey. Our physiotherapists continuously monitor and adjust exercises, ensuring that the rehabilitation process aligns with the evolving needs of each individual.

Choosing Innomitra Elder Physiotherapy means choosing a partner dedicated to promoting independence, enhancing mobility, and nurturing a sense of well-being for your elders.
