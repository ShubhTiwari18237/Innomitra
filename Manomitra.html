Manomitra

Elder Mental Wellness Booklet and Box is a comprehensive and innovative resource designed to prioritize the mental well-being of our senior community members.
Manomitra, meaning "mind friend," encapsulates our commitment to fostering a positive and emotionally fulfilling life for our elders.
This unique booklet and box serve as a holistic approach to addressing mental wellness among older adults.
