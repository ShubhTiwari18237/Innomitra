Meaning of Innomitra

Inno" here is associated with innovation or something new, while "Mitra" is a Sanskrit term that means "friend" or "companion. We are the most helpful and reliable companions for Elder care. At Innomitra, we're reshaping the landscape of elder health & fitness by making it more accessible, efficient, and compassionate, one home at a time. We are a technology-driven elder home health care company dedicated to providing fully personalized care solutions for older individuals, all within the comfort of their own homes. Our innovative concept empowers families to seamlessly arrange, schedule, and oversee elder care, ensuring that their loved ones receive the highest quality of support.

Our Origin

Founded in 2022 by Innobody Systems, Innomitra is headquartered in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. With a commitment to expanding access to high-quality elder care, we have since expanded our footprint and established a network of branch offices spanning across the Madhya Pradesh and India, ensuring our services are within reach for families nationwide.

Our Technology

At the core of Innomitra lies our proprietary technology, designed to deliver transparent and top-tier care to countless service users. Leveraging a user-friendly booking platform and an on-demand digital interface, connecting those in need of in-home assistance with committed skilled & professional caregivers. Our technology allows families to stay informed about a patient's progress, while employing cutting-edge AI to anticipate potential health issues. We offer a wide range of medical care and allied health services, tailored to meet the unique needs of each senior.
