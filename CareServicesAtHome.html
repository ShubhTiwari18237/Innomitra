Elder Care Services At Home

Nursing Care

Our skilled nurses provide professional medical care, ensuring your elders receive the attention and treatment they need. We prioritize their health and safety, offering round-the-clock support

Care Assistance

We believe in the power of companionship. Our caregivers are more than just helpers; they are friends who offer assistance with daily tasks, engage in heartfelt conversations, and provide emotional support.

Physiotherapy

We offer expert physiotherapy sessions designed to improve mobility, reduce pain, and enhance the quality of life for your elders. Our dedicated physiotherapists create tailored plans for each individual.

Yoga

We understand the significance of holistic well-being. Our certified yoga instructors bring the ancient art of yoga to your home, promoting physical fitness, mental balance, and spiritual harmony

Aahar Chikitsa (Nutritional Therapy)

Good health begins with a well-balanced diet. Innomitra provides nutritional therapy services, focusing on dietary choices that support your elders' health, vitality, and longevity.
