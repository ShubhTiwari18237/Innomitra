Elder Care Assistance During Travel

Provided By Innomitra Elder Care

Innomitra Elder Care Provides A Variety Of Services To Help Seniors Travel Safely And Comfortably. These Services Include:

Pre-trip Planning

Innomitra can help you plan your trip, including choosing destinations, making reservations, and packing

In-transit Care

Innomitra can provide caregivers to accompany seniors on flights, trains, and buses. Caregivers can help with tasks such as boarding, disembarking, and navigating airports and train stations

Destination Care

Innomitra can provide caregivers to accompany seniors at their destination. Caregivers can help with tasks such as sightseeing, dining, and shopping

24/7 Support

Innomitra provides 24/7 support to seniors and their families. This support can be accessed by phone or email. Innomitra elder care is committed to providing high-quality care to seniors. The company has a team of experienced and compassionate caregivers who are trained to meet the needs of seniors. Innomitra is also accredited by the Joint Commission on Accreditation of Healthcare Organizations (JCAHO).
