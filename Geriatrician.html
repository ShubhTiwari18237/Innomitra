What is a Geriatrician?

A geriatrician is a doctor who specializes in the care of older adults. Geriatricians have expertise in the medical conditions that are common in older adults, such as Alzheimer's disease, dementia, heart disease, and stroke. They can also provide guidance on how to manage chronic conditions, such as diabetes and arthritis.

What is a Geriatrician Consultation on call?

A Geriatrician Consultation on call is a service that allows seniors to speak with a geriatric doctor by phone or video chat or Physical Visit at home. This service is ideal for seniors who:

Are unable to travel to a doctor's office

Have difficulty hearing or seeing

Need medical advice after hours

Want to talk to a doctor about a new medical condition
